Setup locally:
1. Download the Desktop CMU Graphics module from https://academy.cs.cmu.edu/desktop
2. Unzip the CMU Graphics installer
3. Move the cmu_graphics folder in cmu_graphics_installer next to app.py 
For more details visit https://academy.cs.cmu.edu/desktop

How to play Puzzle Generator: 
Press n to generate a new puzzle
Press h for hints
Click to pick up pieces
Drag to move pieces
Release to place pieces

Notes:
1. pieces may snap into any spot on the puzzle as long as they end up inside the puzzle completely 
and do not overlap egregiously with other puzzle pieces; the class variables in the puzzle class 
can be changed to adjust snapping sensitivity 
2. pieces may be hidden beneath other pieces
